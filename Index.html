Welcome to my professional portfolio. I'm Zachary Neiman-Macak, a dynamic performer who is currently a junior Musical Theatre Major at Pace University. My passion lies in creating art in my movements while focusing on being versatile for numerous leading, supporting and ensemble roles.

With a strong foundation of ten years in gymnastics, I have also been a part of several rock bands, and can be seen in several commercials as well as background work in television shows. Some of my recent credits include Charlie Price in Kinky Boots, Tony in West Side Story, and Finch in Newsies with 5-star Theatreicals.

Apart from performing, I am a certified Pokemon and Nintendo enthusiast, a firm ally of the Taco Bell Crunchwrap Supreme, and a man who loves creating original music and rock climbing.
